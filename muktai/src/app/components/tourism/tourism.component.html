Saint Muktabai Temple:

Saint Muktabai Temple is a region's ancient temple with Doddess Muktabai beimg honoured at diety of region. There are two temples of deity namely Mehun temple and new Muktabai Temple located in MuktaiNagar city. Muktai and Muktabai was a very popular saint of the Varkari tradition. She was born in Deshastha Bramhin family as the younger sister of the first Varkari Saint, Dhyaneshswar. She wrote around 41 Abhang (devotional songs) all through her life. Muktabai's most popular and throughful work is "Tati ughada dhyaneshswara" which is piece of conversation of saint Dhyaneshswar. Muktabai always believed that a saint is the one who open to or can accept criticisms from others, that's why she said "Sant jene vahave jag bolane sosave".
